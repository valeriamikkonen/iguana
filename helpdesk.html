Frequently Asked Questions

Iguana

Iguana is the perfect way to develop your professional skills and grow in your career. It is an online education page that offers courses from management to communication and marketing.
To follow a course, simply register or log in, and select the course you want from the courses tab.

How do I subscribe to Iguana?
We are not a subscription based platform. You simply pay only for what you want. No additional fees.

Prices

Unfortunately, at the moment, we don’t offer returns.

Do you have discounts for companies?
Yes! If you are interested in purchasing packages for your employees, contact us and we will give you the details.

Courses

How can I find the course I was watching?
If you want to get back to the courses you have already finished, or continue watching the ones you haven’t finished, you can find them in your profile.
